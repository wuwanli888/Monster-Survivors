Monster Survivors

Survive the onslaught of monsters in this thrilling roguelike adventure!

About The Game

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating roguelike survival game where you face waves of increasingly challenging monsters. Collect power-ups, level up your character, and see how long you can survive in this action-packed adventure.

As time passes, the difficulty increases, requiring strategic choices about which upgrades to select and how to navigate the monster-filled battlefield. Will you focus on offensive abilities or defensive tactics? The choice is yours!

Game Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Mouse: Aim your attacks
  • Space: Special ability (when available)
  • P: Pause game

Game Features

Roguelike Progression

Each run is unique with procedurally generated levels and random power-ups, providing endless replayability.

Character Evolution

Level up your character and choose from a variety of abilities and upgrades to create your own unique playstyle.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a wide array of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns that will test your reflexes.

Tips and Strategies

Mastering Monster Survivors requires both quick reflexes and thoughtful strategy. Here are some tips to help you survive longer:

  • Focus on movement speed early to help avoid monsters more effectively
  • Try to maintain consistent damage output by balancing offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Don't get cornered! Always have an escape route planned
  • Prioritize eliminating more dangerous monster types first
  • Experiment with different upgrade paths to discover powerful synergies
