Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed roguelike game. Collect power-ups, level up, and become the ultimate survivor!

Game Features

Endless Action

Face waves of increasingly challenging monsters in an endless battle for survival.

Power-Up System

Collect unique power-ups and combine abilities to create powerful synergies.

Character Progress

Level up your character and unlock new abilities as you progress through the game.

How to Play

  • 1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • 2. Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • 3. Collect experience gems to level up
  • 4. Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when leveling up

Tips and Strategies

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by enemies
  • Focus on complementary abilities that work well together
  • Prioritize collecting experience gems to level up quickly
  • Try to maintain a safe distance while your attacks deal damage
